Feedback:

Session Rate and Cost may be redundant depending on what they mean.
If Cost = SessionRate × SessionQty, then Cost is a calculated value and usually does not need to be stored.
You may also want a ServiceType field, such as:
30-minute walk
60-minute walk
pet sitting

Better option

Add PetID as a foreign key in Services if each session is for one specific pet
